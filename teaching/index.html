Teaching

Frankfurt School of Finance & Management

I taught the introductory Mathematics course in the Bachelor in the Business Administration program in Fall 2017 and Fall 2018. The course covered linear algebra, optimization, and differential and integral calculus.

In Fall 2022, I taught a portion of the Foundations of Finance course in the Master of Finance program and conducted tutorial sessions involving problem set solutions. As the title of the course suggests, the course covered the fundamentals of corporate finance and asset valuation. These include the valuation of stocks, bonds, and vanilla derivatives, an introduction to mean-variance portfolios, the Capital Asset Pricing Model, and the efficient market hypothesis, as well as capital budgeting and payout policies.
