Emirhan İlhan

I am an Assistant Professor in the Department of Finance at the National University of Singapore (NUS) Business School. I am also a Research Affiliate at the NUS Sustainable and Green Finance Institute (SGFIN) and a Research Fellow at the NUS Risk Management Institute (RMI).

My research interests lie in climate finance, with applications in household finance, corporate finance, and asset pricing.
